About the University Hospital Haiti Health

About University Hospital Haiti Health University Hospital is an acute care facility serving as the major referral center for southwest Alabama, southeast Mississippi and portions of northwest Florida. We offer centers for Level I trauma, burn, stroke, cardiovascular disease and sickle cell disease. As a teaching and research facility for the University of South Alabama College of Medicine, University Hospital plays a key role in the development of new technology, treatments and training of future healthcare professionals. The hospital also includes outpatient care services such as cardiology, medicine and surgery. University Hospital is a member of the American Hospital Association.

Services Offered at University Hospital Haiti Health

Adult Infectious Disease

Our infectious disease specialists treat patients with various disorders caused by germs. Infectious diseases include measles and chickenpox—which can be prevented by vaccines—meningitis, Lyme disease, malaria, E.coli and HIV/AIDS.

Adult Intensive Care

The intensive care unit (ICU) is, in some ways, an extensive of the Fanny Meisler Trauma Center. Once a trauma patients immediate needs have been addressed and their health is stabilized, they can be transferred to our adult ICU for further critical care.

Capsule Endoscopy

With this minimally invasive technology—also known as camera in a pill—doctors examine and diagnose disorders of the gastrointestinal tract without using sedation.

Bariatric Surgery

Bariatric surgery, also known as weight-loss surgery, includes a variety of procedures to help patients lose weight and improve their overall health.

Burn Surgery

HAITI Health surgeons provide multiple options for repairing third-degree burns, including artificial skin.

Cardio-Oncology

With this program, our cardiology team help patients undergoing cancer treatment to manage medications that can affect the heart.

Cardiology Outpatient Testing

At our outpatient testing facility, patients undergo stress tests, cardiac monitoring, Doppler testing to measure blood flow through arteries and veins, and other diagnostic procedures.

Colonoscopy

Colonoscopy is a procedure to search the colon and rectum for possible growths or other irregularities.

Colorectal Surgery

Focuses on using surgical procedures to treat diseases of the adrenal gland, thyroid and parathyroi

Arnold Luterman Regional Burn Center

Arnold Luterman Regional Burn Center is the only facility in the region providing burn care to patients from the time of their injury to recovery. The burn team is comprised of physicians specializing in burn surgery, infectious disease, plastic surgery, trauma surgery, orthopaedic surgery, psychiatry and anesthesiology. Our Burn Center research team is nationally and internationally recognized, particularly in the development of artificial skin. Team members contribute to hundreds of medical publications, making significant contributions in virtually every aspect of burn care. Learn more about our Burn Care services below.

Fanny Meisler Trauma Center

The Fanny Meisler Trauma Center at USA Health is the only Level I trauma center (prevention through recovery with advanced surgical care) in southwest Alabama, indicating the highest level of care a facility can provide. We believe that the highest level of care is the only level of care anyone would expect to receive in a life-threatening situation. No matter what day a patient arrives, we have a board-certified trauma surgeon in-house 24 hours a day. Every trauma patient who is brought in is assigned a designated trauma team composed of a trauma surgeon, emergency physician, surgical residents, trauma nurse coordinator, emergency department nurses, radiologic technologist, respiratory therapists, and an anesthesiologist or nurse anesthetist. Learn more about our Trauma services below.
